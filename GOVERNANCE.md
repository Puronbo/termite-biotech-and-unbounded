# Governance

This project operates under a decentralized, consent-based governance model.

For the complete governance framework — including roles (Gardeners, Wayfinders, Chroniclers, Stewards), decision-making process, revenue sharing, and forking policy — see:

> **[unbounded/GOVERNANCE.md](./unbounded/GOVERNANCE.md)**

### Summary

- **No gatekeeping** — anyone can participate
- **Consent-based decisions** — proposals proceed unless someone blocks with a reasoned objection
- **Roles, not titles** — all roles are rotating and temporary
- **Forking is a feature** — if you disagree, fork. The idea survives the institution.
